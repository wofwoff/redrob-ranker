# does not want. Similarity to these pushes a candidate DOWN.

curious about how AI tools could augment my work and I have experimented with
ChatGPT and a few other tools for productivity and content creation. Open to
roles in the AI space. My skills list includes many trending AI keywords.

Pure research profile. I spent my career in academic labs and research-only
roles publishing papers. I have deep theoretical knowledge but no production
deployment, no shipping to real users, and no product-company experience.

My AI experience is recent and consists mostly of LangChain tutorials wiring up
calls to OpenAI over the last few months. I build demos with the latest hot
framework and write blog posts about them, but I have little pre-LLM machine
learning or production systems experience.

I have spent my entire career at large IT services and consulting firms doing
staffing-style project delivery for clients. I have not worked at a product
company and have not owned a system end to end for my own users.

Computer vision and speech specialist. My work is object detection, image
classification, YOLO, OpenCV, and speech recognition. I have no natural language
processing, information retrieval, ranking, or search experience.

Senior architect and tech lead. I have not written production code in years; I
review designs, run ceremonies, and manage delivery. My title has climbed as I
